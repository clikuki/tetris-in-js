Controls

  • A - Move left
  • D - Move right
  • Q - Rotate left
  • E - Rotate right
  • S - Soft drop/manual lock
  • Spacebar - Hard drop
  • F - Hold tetromino
  • R - Restart on game over

Scoring system

  • Single - 100
  • Double - 300
  • Triple - 500
  • Tetris - 800
  • Combo - 50 * Combo count
  • T-spin - same as double cause i cant be bothered to check for t spins
