Controls

  • A - Move left
  • D - Move right
  • K - Rotate left
  • L - Rotate right
  • S - Soft drop/manual lock
  • Spacebar - Hard drop
  • F - Hold tetromino
  • R - Restart on game over
  • P - Pause

Scoring system

  • Single - 100 × level
  • Double - 300 × level
  • Triple - 500 × level
  • Tetris - 800 × level
  • T-spin single - 800 × level
  • T-spin double - 1200 × level
  • T-spin triple - 1600 × level
  • Back-to-Back Tetris/T-spin - Original added points × 1.5
  • Combo - 50 * Combo count × level
